Our Mission

Lowering the barrier to professional music production worldwide.

What is PluggedIn?

PluggedIn Studio is a global marketplace for music creators. We connect artists and producers with high-quality recording spaces—ranging from professional commercial facilities to unique home studios.

Our platform is designed to make the process of finding, booking, and managing studio sessions as seamless as possible, so creators can focus on what matters most: the music.

Why we built it

The music industry is evolving. Access to professional gear and treated environments shouldn't be limited by local connections or massive budgets. We're building the infrastructure for the next generation of independent music production.
