Cookie Policy

Everything you need to know about how we use cookies

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

PluggedIn Studio uses cookies for several reasons:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in and secure your account.
  • Preferences: To remember your Dark Mode/Light Mode settings.
  • Performance: To understand how users interact with our platform and improve load times.
  • Analytics: To track platform usage and high-performing studios.

3. Managing Your Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.
