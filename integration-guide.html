How to Set Up Integrations

1

Access Your Studio Dashboard

Navigate to your studio owner dashboard and click on the "Calendar Integrations" tab.

Go to Dashboard
2

Choose Your Platform

Select the calendar or booking system you want to integrate from the available options.

Tip: You can integrate multiple platforms simultaneously for maximum flexibility.

3

Provide Authentication

Depending on the platform, you'll either:

  • OAuth: Click "Connect Account" and authorize PluggedIn to access your calendar
  • API Key: Enter your API credentials from your platform's settings

Security: All credentials are encrypted and stored securely. We never store your passwords.

4

Configure Sync Settings

Choose what data to sync:

  • Sync availability to PluggedIn
  • Sync bookings from external system
  • Prevent double bookings
‚úì

Test & Go Live

Test the integration to ensure everything works correctly, then start accepting bookings from both platforms!

Frequently Asked Questions

How often does the sync happen?

Most integrations sync in real-time using webhooks. For platforms without webhook support, we sync every 15 minutes.

What happens if there's a conflict?

Our system prevents double bookings by checking availability across all connected platforms before confirming any booking.

Can I use multiple calendar systems?

Yes! You can integrate as many calendar systems as you need. All will be synchronized to prevent conflicts.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All API keys and tokens are encrypted and stored securely. We follow industry best practices for data protection.

What if I need help?

Our support team is here to help! Contact us through the dashboard or email support@pluggedin.studio.
